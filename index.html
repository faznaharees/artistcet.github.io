About Us

ARTIFEX is an initiative taken by the College Union in the effort to foster a college driven by creativity that provides the opportunity for everyone to engage in diverse arts and culture. We believe that great artisanship inspires us, brings us together and teaches about ourselves and the world around us.


The primary vision of the gallery is to procure, propose & promote an exclusive range of Contemporary Art, Modern Art and all other genres of Painting by exploring and evaluating artists. This online gallery showcases some of the finest collections of arts, crafts and painting of our eminent art experts.

Peruse and Purchase Art online

ARTIFEX expose a wide array of Contemporary & Modern Art & Paintings Online. Every piece of artwork displayed on the website is chosen by our crew to be put on site along with the artiste’s profile. These diverse pieces of artwork are sure to stick out and catch the eyes of the insightful art collector or even a first-time art buyer.

The paintings are of different mediums like – Oil Paintings, Acrylic Paintings, Watercolour Paintings, Graphite and Charcoal sketches.

ARTIFEX showcases artworks on various style & subjects like – Abstract Paintings, Landscape Paintings, Portrait Paintings, Caricature works, Mandala art forms etc.
